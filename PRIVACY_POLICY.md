# Privacy Policy for MizCall

**Last Updated: January 16, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to MizCall ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our video calling application and services.

By using MizCall, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you register and use our services:

- **Account Information**: Username, email address, password (encrypted), display name, and avatar/profile picture
- **Authentication Data**: Two-factor authentication settings and verification codes
- **Contact Information**: Email address for account recovery and important notifications

### 1.2 Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type, device identifiers, IP address, and device tokens for push notifications
- **Session Information**: Login timestamps, session duration, active sessions, and device details
- **Call Metadata**: Call duration, participant information, timestamps, room IDs, and connection quality metrics

### 1.3 Media Content
- **Audio and Video**: During video calls, we process audio and video streams in real-time. We only store recordings when explicitly initiated by authorized hosts
- **Call Backgrounds**: Custom background images you choose to upload or use
- **Recordings**: Video/audio recordings of calls when recording is enabled (stored with your explicit consent)

### 1.4 Usage Information
- **Performance Metrics**: Application performance data, error logs, and system diagnostics
- **Activity Logs**: Feature usage, navigation patterns, and interaction data to improve our services

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Service Provision
- Creating and managing your account
- Facilitating video and audio calls
- Enabling host and participant features
- Processing and storing call recordings (when authorized)
- Providing customer support

### 2.2 Security and Authentication
- Verifying your identity
- Implementing two-factor authentication
- Managing single device enforcement settings
- Detecting and preventing fraud and unauthorized access
- Maintaining security logs

### 2.3 Communication
- Sending important service notifications
- Providing call-related notifications
- Delivering push notifications for incoming calls
- Sending system updates and security alerts

### 2.4 Service Improvement
- Analyzing usage patterns to improve features
- Monitoring performance and identifying issues
- Conducting research and development
- Generating anonymous analytics and statistics

### 2.5 Legal Compliance
- Complying with legal obligations
- Enforcing our terms of service
- Protecting our rights and property
- Resolving disputes

## 3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

### 3.1 Data Storage
- Your data is stored in secure databases with encryption
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Call recordings are stored securely and accessible only to authorized users
- Session data is maintained with appropriate security measures

### 3.2 Data Security Measures
We implement comprehensive security measures including:
- End-to-end encryption for call data in transit
- Encrypted storage for sensitive information
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Access controls and authentication requirements
- Automated database backups
- Secure API endpoints with authentication

### 3.3 Data Retention
- Active account data is retained while your account is active
- Session information is retained for security purposes
- Call recordings are retained until you choose to delete them
- System logs are retained for operational and security purposes
- Deleted account data is permanently removed within 30 days

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to:
- Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
- Protection of our rights or property
- Prevention of fraud or security threats
- Compliance with regulatory requirements

### 4.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

### 4.4 With Your Consent
We may share your information with third parties when you provide explicit consent.
